Finish swatches are CSS approximations for quick visual reference only. 626, 630, and 619 can appear nearly identical on screen — in person, 630 reads distinctly cooler/bluer, 626 has a warm undertone, and 619 reads notably warmer/grey. Similarly, 605 and 629 are very close on screen but differ in tone. For living finishes (609, 613), variation between manufacturers and over time is significant.
